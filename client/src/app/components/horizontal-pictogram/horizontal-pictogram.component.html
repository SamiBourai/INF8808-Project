First, let's compare the 7 teams in terms of their rosters. Initially, in terms of theoretical quality. For this, we represent in white, the players who play in a club that finished in the top 5 of one of the 5 major leagues (Bundesliga, La Liga, Serie A, Premier League, Ligue 1) during the 2021-2022 season. These players play more important matches and are accustomed to the intensity of very high-level matches needed to perform in the World Cup. Players playing elsewhere are in black. We observe that only 5 players from Morocco play at this level against 20 for France and even 11 in Senegal.

By clicking on the toggle button, the view changes to a univariate scatterplot that orders players by their age and also shows the average. We can see that Morocco has a younger squad on average than the Top 3 teams and even has the youngest player of the 7 teams studied.

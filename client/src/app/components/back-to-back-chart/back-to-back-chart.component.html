Scored and Conceded Goals

In this specific visualization, the teams' performances are compared side by side, with Morocco, the surprising fourth-placed team, placed in direct comparison with the world-class teams of Argentina (the champions), France (2nd place), and Croatia (3rd place) and its african peers.

The length of the bars represents the quantity of goals scored or conceded, with each bar's direction indicating whether it pertains to goals scored or goals conceded. By analyzing this chart, we can discern key insights about Morocco's performance during the tournament. The balance between offensive and defensive performance, as well as how these aspects compare with the top three teams, can be understood at a glance.

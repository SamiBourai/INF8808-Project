Morocco :
Story Of An Outsider That Reached The Semis

During the last FIFA World Cup the Morocco National Football team shocks that world qualifying for the semi-final for the first time of its history.

On this page, we come back on the competition of "The Atlas Lions" that make vibrate the heart of thousands around the globe. You'll encounter of this page, several visualization that decrypt the team and its performances.

To better understand the profil of the national football team of Morocco, we decided to compare it to the 3 other semi-finalist (Top3) : Argentina, France, Croatia. And also the 3 closest qualified African teams in the FiFA National Rank (African Countries) : Senegal, Tunisia, Ghana.

Whether you are a football enthousiast, a "Atlas Lions" supporter, a football professionnal, or just interested by the subject, this page is dedicated to help you visualize the key components that helped Morocco to reach the 4th place of the competition.

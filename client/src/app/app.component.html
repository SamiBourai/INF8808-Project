Morocco:
The Story of an Outsider that Reached the Semifinals


During the last FIFA World Cup, the Morocco National Football team surprised the world by qualifying for the semifinals for the first time in its history, defeating top-tier teams.

On this page, we revisit the journey of "The Atlas Lions" that captivated thousands around the globe. You will find various visualizations on this page that analyze the team and its performances.

To gain a better understanding of the Morocco national football team, we compare it to the other three semifinalists (Top 3): Argentina, France, and Croatia. Additionally, we compare it to the three closest African teams in the FIFA National Rank (Similar African Nations): Senegal, Tunisia, and Ghana.

Whether you are a football enthusiast, a supporter of the "Atlas Lions," a football professional, or simply interested in the subject, this page is dedicated to helping you visualize the key components that led Morocco to achieve the 4th place in the competition.



Throughout this website, the same color code is used to distinguish Morocco from the two comparison groups: the Top 3 (Argentina, France, Croatia) and the Similar African Nations (Senegal, Tunisia, Ghana).
